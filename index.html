Tour du Mont Blanc

My favorite trek

100+ mile alpine hike around the Mont-Blanc massif through France, Switzerland, and Italy

Erin and I started our trek in the small French village of Les Houches, after a trans-Atlantic red-eye into Geneva.

It didn't take long for the jaw-dropping views to begin.

Competing for attention were the charming alpine villages we wandered through day after day.

We brought a tent... But after one rainy, cold night we decided to enjoy the refuges and auberges along the trail. Demi-pension included a bunk in a shared room, a warm dinner, and usually breakfast.

We walked between 18 and 25 miles each day.

There was always something to point my camera at - from far away to close up.
